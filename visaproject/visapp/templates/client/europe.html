Europe visa

VISITOR VISA / TOURIST VISA / BUSINESS VISA


Document

  1. Latest Valid Passport and Old Passports (if applicable)
  2. Three Photographs Latest (35 x 45mm, white background, 80% face cover)
  3. Original Saving A/c Bank Statements of last 3 months with sign and seal
  4. Copy of the Income Tax Papers for last 3 years
  5. Overseas Travel Health Insurance ( At least 30,000 Euros or Higher)
  6. Fixed Deposits, Investments (If any)
  7. Confirm return Air ticket
  8. Proofs of Accommodation (Hotel Reservation / Private Individual), Travel plan/itinerary
  9. Foreign exchange or International credit card (Optional)
  10. If sponsored by somebody from Schengen for a tourist visa then the consulate may require original sponsorship documents
  11. if job
    1. Salary Slips of last 3 months
    2. Leave Sanction Letter
  12. if Business
    1. Proofs of Company Registration, Visiting Cards, Covering letter on Letterhead
    2. Original Current A/c Bank Statements of last 3 months with sign and seal

Business Visitors

• Official Business Invitation Letter from the European company

• Copy of Passport / National ID Proof of the person who is inviting
